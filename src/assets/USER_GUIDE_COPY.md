# Darts League - Quick Start Guide

**Welcome to the Oche! Here's how to get started:**

### 1. Sign In
Head to the sidebar and click **Sign In**. While anyone can view the Venue Dashboard, you need an account to track stats, view leaderboards, and join tournaments. Ask a Coordinator if you need an invite!

### 2. The Dashboard
Your central hub! Here you can see active tournaments, recent match results, and track your overall season progress.

### 3. Track Your Stats
Navigate to the **Players** section to check out detailed statistics including your Win/Loss record, 3-dart average, checkout percentage, and highest scores. See how you stack up against the competition!

### 4. Tournament Play
When it's league night, head to the **Tournaments** tab. Find your upcoming match in the bracket, step up to the board, and let the darts fly.

### 5. Scoring Made Easy
Use the built-in Scorer for your matches! It supports X01 (with single/double/triple out rules) and Cricket. It even features a full throw history so you can undo mistakes.

**Look out for special Syndicate Mode events at partner venues!**
