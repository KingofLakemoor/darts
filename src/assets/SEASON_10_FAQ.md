# Darts League Season 10 FAQs

## Format
Tournaments will be held in various formats including Single Elimination, Double Elimination, and Round Robin. We will feature both X01 (301, 501, 701) and Cricket (Standard, Cut Throat) game types throughout the season. Competitors are encouraged to sign up early for each event.

## Syndicate Mode
Special 'Syndicate Mode' tournaments will be held at official Syndicate Partner venues. These events feature unique styling and may carry additional prestige or distinct rulesets for the night.

## Scoring and Rules
*   **X01:** Rules for checking in and out (Single, Double, or Triple) will vary by tournament. Please check the specific tournament details before your match.
*   **Cricket:** Standard and Cut Throat variants will be played. Pay attention to the tournament configuration to see if random numbers are in effect.

## Standings and Seeding
Player stats and standings are tracked throughout the season. Tournament brackets may be seeded randomly, by season performance (wins), or by skill level (average score). Accumulate wins to improve your seeding in future tournaments!

## Accounts and Access
Anyone can view the Venue Dashboard, but you must sign in to view Player Stats, Tournaments, and Leaderboards. Reach out to a Coordinator if you need an account created or linked.
